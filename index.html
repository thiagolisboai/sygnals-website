Intent Score

One number that tells you who's ready.

Sygnals weighs four signals into a single 0–100 score, so you always know who to message first.

Hz

Frequency

How often they engage with relevant content. More activity, more intent.

Sr

Seniority

Decision-makers weigh heavier. A VP comment beats ten interns.

Ty

Engagement type

A thoughtful comment signals more than a passive like.

Rc

Recency

Fresh signals score higher. Old activity decays automatically.
